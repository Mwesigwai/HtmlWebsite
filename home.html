About The North Farm

The North Farm was founded in 2020 by a group of three people.
At that time, there was a terrible food crisis. People had money but could not find food to buy because there we no farmers at that village.
Many people were occupied by other activities with a hope that others would grow crops and they would buy from them.
Unfortunately, all of the people at that village thought the same thing therefore everyone abandoned farming.
Food became very expensive and scarse yet there was enough land for growing crops. This was the begining of an idea to start the north farm by the three wise men.
North farm was started and we crops on large scale to an extent of a suplus
